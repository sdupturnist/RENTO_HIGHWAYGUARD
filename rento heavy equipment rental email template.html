Many heavy equipment and transport rental operations lose thousands in revenue due to Underutilization of equipment and operators, double bookings, manual timesheet issues, vehicle breakdown due to missed maintenances, and expired equipment permits.

RentO is a specialized ERP designed to eliminate these operational leaks. It gives you real-time visibility on availability and utilization details of your heavy machineries and operators, it helps to create assignments, generate time sheets for client approval and generate invoices.
